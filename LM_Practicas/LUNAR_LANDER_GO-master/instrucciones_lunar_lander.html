"LUNAR LANDER"

Game instructions :
    Land the ship on the moon surface.
    The maximum allowed speed is: 4 m / s.
  Game keys:
    - Press the space key to activate the ship.
    - With a click you will keep the ship's engine on.
    - Push the rocket to change difficulty's level.
